Project Descriptions

Applications are invited for a fully-funded Ph.D. position in the College of Engineering and Computer Science (CECS) at VinUniversity. These positions are closely co-advised by Dr. Binh Nguyen (Assistant Professor, CECS) and Dr. Khoa Doan (Assistant Professor, CECS and Associate Director at VinUni-Illinois Smart Health Center).

The research will focus on developing and analyzing novel algorithms in safe and responsible AI models, especially on Large Language Models (LLMs), Vision-Language Models, and agent-based environments. Topics of interest include (but are not limited to):

  • Low-resource fine-tuning techniques.
  • Mechanistic interpretability.
  • Statistical inference methods for LLMs evaluation.
  • Uncertainty quantification of Large Language Models.
  • Game-theoretic and statistical analysis of LLM agents.

with potential applications in sustainability, farming, material discovery, and healthcare.

Key Qualifications

Applicants should hold (or be close to completing) a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.

You might be a good fit if you:

  • Have experience with machine learning and/or AI safety research.
  • Have a strong technical background in computer science, mathematics, physics, or related fields.
  • Have strong programming skills, particularly in Python and machine learning frameworks.
  • Demonstrate the ability to independently execute your project while incorporating feedbacks from other collaborators.
  • (Big plus) Have experience in submitting at top international venues (A*).

Even if you don’t have 100% of the skills listed above, please feel free to apply for the job.

Benefits

  • This position is fully funded and offers a competitive stipend and full tuition support, research support, and travel funding to present at leading conferences.
  • Access to computing resources and research infrastructure.
  • Opportunities for collaboration with international partners, including the University of Notre Dame, UIUC, CISPA, MPI, UMass, University of Stuttgart, Virginia Tech, etc…
  • For candidates without a Master Degree, opportunities to spend 2 years at a partner university to complete the Master Degree.
  • A vibrant and growing AI research environment at VinUniversity in Hanoi, Vietnam

HOW TO APPLY

Applicants can send an email to binh.nt2[at]vinuni.edu.vn, or khoa.dd@vinuni.edu.vn or directly fill in THIS FORM.
