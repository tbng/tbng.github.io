About

I obtained my PhD in Proba-Stat team of Département de Mathématiques d’Orsay and INRIA Parietal team. I was very fortunate to be advised by Sylvain Arlot and Bertrand Thirion. I worked with statistical inference in high-dimension, on both theoretical and algorithmic sides.

Lately, I have developed an interest in the application of optimal transport to machine learning, in particular to structured data, and will soon join LTCI at Telecom Paristech as a Postdoc to work in this area.

I am also an advocate of the usage of open-source software and reproducible science. Checkout here and here for the open-source projects that I have been developing/contributing to.
