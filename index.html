About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at LTCI - Telecom Paristech, working with Florence d’Alché, Gabriel Peyré and Remi Flamary on the applications of optimal transport to machine learning, in particular to graph-structured data using Gromov-Wasserstein distance (and its variants).

I obtained my PhD in Proba-Stat team of Département de Mathématiques d’Orsay and INRIA Parietal team. I was very fortunate to be advised by Sylvain Arlot and Bertrand Thirion. I worked with statistical inference in high-dimension, on both theoretical and algorithmic sides.

I spent a fair amount of time during my PhD for the developments of open-source softwares, and hope to continue to do so in the future. Checkout here and here for the open-source projects that I have been developing/contributing to.
