About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at LTCI - Telecom Paristech, working on the applications of optimal transport to machine learning, in particular to structured data. I am also interest in kernel methods and neural differential equations.

I obtained my PhD in Proba-Stat team of Département de Mathématiques d’Orsay and INRIA Parietal team. I was very fortunate to be advised by Sylvain Arlot and Bertrand Thirion. I worked with statistical inference in high-dimension, on both theoretical and algorithmic sides.

I spent a fair amount of time during my PhD for the developments of open-source softwares. Checkout here and here for the open-source projects that I have been developing/contributing to.
